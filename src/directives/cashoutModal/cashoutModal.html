Cash Out
Your pension fund is simply paid to you in one go.
Advantages
You can use the money for whatever you want
Disadvantages
75% of your fund will be taxed
You have no future income
If you have other income the tax charge will be higher.
HMRC will usually assume a one-off lump sum is the start of a regular monthly income. This means you could pay too much tax initially and you will need to contact HRMC to reclaim the overpaid tax.
