Option selection
Annuity
A friendly Lemonade adviser will discuss your requirements and search the market for the best rate. They will also ask questions regarding your state of health, as any issues could increase your income. Finally, they will arrange the transfer from your pension scheme.
What does it cost?
The typical cost is 1.5% of your fund to select and set up your annuity. The 1.5% is applied to the remaining fund after you have taken your tax free cash.
How do I pay?
The cost can be taken from your pension pot, or you can pay directly if you prefer.
