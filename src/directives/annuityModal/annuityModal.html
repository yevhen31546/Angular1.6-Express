ANNUITY
You pay your pension fund to an insurance company and in return they pay you a guaranteed income for life.
Advantages
Certainty - your income will last your whole life
It's automatic and does not need to be managed
Disadvantages
Cannot be changed once started
If you die soon after starting an annuity, you may receive less back than the pension fund you exchanged to buy it

Annuity Options
You can add options to an annuity, for example to increase the amount each year or to ensure it carries on being paid to your spouse after you die. However, each option will reduce your starting income.
Adding an increasing income and full spouse's pension could decrease your income by 45%
Enhanced Annuities
If you belong to any of the following groups, you may be able to get a higher retirement income:
  • High cholesterol
  • Diabetes
  • Smoker
  • High blood pressure
  • Obesity
  • Certain postcodes
Your income could increase by as much as 50%
