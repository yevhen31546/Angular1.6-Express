Retirement Options Planner

Before we start, it would help if you have the following information to hand:

1. How many pensions you have
2. What types of pension you have (e.g. Money Purchase or Final Salary)
3. The current value of each pension
4. What % per month you pay into your pension
5. What % per month your employer pays into your pension
6. How much state pension you will get – if you don’t know find out here
7. An idea of how much money you want / need in retirement
